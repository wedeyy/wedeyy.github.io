The difference between duct tape and ordinary duct tape

The differences between cloth-based tape and ordinary tape are mainly reflected in the material composition, performance characteristics and application scenarios.

1.Ingredients:

The cloth base adhesive tape is mainly composed of cloth (such as cotton cloth, polyester cloth, etc.) , the surface is coated with a strong adhesive. 1 ordinary adhesive tape is mainly made of plastic film (such as PVC, PET, etc.) and coated with adhesive.
