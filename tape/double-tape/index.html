What's the difference between static tape and regular tape?

tape double tape
Publish Date:   2024-07-13

There are significant differences between electrostatic tape and ordinary tape in a number of aspects, the following is a detailed analysis of these differences:

First, the working principle

Electrostatic tape: electrostatic tape is the use of the principle of mutual attraction of oppositely charged work. In the moment of tearing the tape, the tape front and back contact surface will occur charge transfer, making the tape on the two surfaces with different charges, and then use the attraction of the charge to achieve the role of the tape. This type of tape relies on electrostatic self-adhesion rather than traditional adhesives.
Ordinary tape: Ordinary tape adheres to objects by means of an adhesive on its surface. The adhesive has a good viscosity, and can be firmly stuck to the items that need to be fixed.

Second, the use of characteristics

Adhesive ability: Static tape can mainly stick to the tape itself, for most other items, especially with conductive items, its adhesive ability is weak. While the ordinary tape can stick to a variety of items including skin, hair, has a stronger adhesion ability.
Anti-static function: Static tape has the function of anti-static, which can effectively prevent the generation and accumulation of static electricity. This is especially important for some static sensitive products or areas. The ordinary tape does not have the function of anti-static.

Application Scenarios

Static tape: Because of its anti-static properties, static tape is usually used for packaging, fixing and marking of anti-static areas or static-sensitive products. In addition, electrostatic tape can also be used on the ground, workshop or floor as a signpost, to play a warning and management role.
Ordinary tape: The application range of ordinary tape is much wider, and it can be used for all kinds of pasting, fixing and encapsulation needs in daily life, such as sealing, packaging, repairing and so on.

Material and durability

Static tape: static tape is generally made of PVC and other anti-static materials, wear-resistant, alkali resistance, oil resistance and other characteristics, can be used in a more adverse environment. At the same time, because of its anti-static properties, static tape also has a certain stability and moisture resistance.
General Tape: The material and durability of general tape varies depending on the specific type. But generally speaking, ordinary tape also has a certain degree of abrasion resistance, water resistance and other characteristics, to meet the general needs of use.

V. Summary

There are obvious differences between electrostatic tape and ordinary tape in terms of working principle, usage characteristics, application scenarios, as well as material and durability. With its unique static self-adhesive properties and anti-static function, the electrostatic tape has a wide range of applications in specific fields and scenarios; while the ordinary tape plays an important role in daily life with its wide range of application and strong adhesive ability. When choosing to use, you should select the right type of tape according to the specific needs and scenarios.
